2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Local game data — Your puzzle progress, in‑game settings, scores and achievements are stored locally on your device unless you enable cloud backup (see below).
  • Device & technical data — Device model, operating system version, app version, language/locale, screen size and basic diagnostics (crash logs, performance metrics).
  • Identifiers — Advertising identifiers such as Google Advertising ID (GAID) may be accessed by third‑party ad and analytics SDKs. We do not link advertising identifiers to personal contact information in the App.
  • Analytics & crash reporting — Aggregate and anonymous usage metrics and crash reports to help us improve the App.
  • Ads data (if ads enabled) — Third‑party advertising networks may collect device identifiers and technical signals to serve and measure ads.

3. How we collect information

  • Automatically: Device & usage data are collected automatically by the App and/or third‑party SDKs.
  • Locally: Game progress and settings are stored on your device by default.
  • Cloud backup (optional): If you enable an optional cloud backup feature (for example to transfer progress between devices), your backup will be stored in a secure cloud storage service — details shown on the backing screen. Enabling backup is optional and requires your explicit confirmation.

4. Purpose of collection

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the App.
  • To analyze usage patterns, diagnose crashes, and improve features (analytics & crash reporting).
  • To show advertisements (if enabled) and measure ad performance.
  • To provide optional cloud backup and restore of game progress when you enable it.
